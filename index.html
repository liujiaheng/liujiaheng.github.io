Biography

I obtained a PhD degree (2019-2023) in the Department of Computer Science, Beihang University, advised by Prof. Ke Xu and Prof. Dong Xu. I have closely collaborated with Dr. Yichao Wu, Dr. Jinyang Guo, Dr. Tan Yu, and Prof. Wanli Ouyang. My research interests include computer vision and natural language processing. I have done related research works on knowledge distillation, neural architecture search, face recognition, point cloud understanding, multi-modal learning, and large language models.

I obtained a bachelor's degree (2015-2019) from the Department of Software Engineering, Beihang University.

I interned at SenseTime (working with Dr. Yichao Wu), Baidu (working with Dr. Tan Yu), and Shanghai AI Lab (working with Dr. Tong He). Recently, I have been working on Large Language Models at Alibaba.
